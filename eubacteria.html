Fun Facts!

  1. They have a cell wall that is lined with plasma.

  2. Get their nutrients from three key sources, the sun, organic, and inorganic compounds.

  3. Types of eubacteria cause cholera, typhus, lyme's, salmonellosis, etc.

  4. Some types are nitrogren fixing which helps keep the right help of nitrogen in the atmosphere.

  5. Antibiotics work by disrupting the functioning bacterial ribosomes or creation of cell walls, which keeps them from multiplying.

  6. Some types are used in the making of curd, yogurt, cheese, coy sauce, and more.

  7. Asexual and sexual reproduction has both been observed.
