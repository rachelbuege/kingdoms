The category we humans fit into!

All animalia members are multicellular, and a major thing that sets us apart is the lack of cell walls that plants have. Estimated there are about 9 to 10 million animal species on Earth. The origin of the name animal comes from Latin animalis, which means ‘having soul’, ‘living being’, and ‘having breath’.
