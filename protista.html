Now some confusing critters!

This kingdom is incredibly large and diverse in terms of genetics and ecological diversity. They habitat in every ecosystem and key components of biogeochemical cycles and trophic webs. There does not exist a single accepted definition for them, they have a range of biological characteristics.




Fun Facts!

  1. Some protists are single-celled, some are syncytial, and some are mutlicellular.

  2. Algae are a major protist type

  3. A lot of protsits are pathogens to humans.

  4. Malaria is caused by the protist Plasmodium falciparum.

  5. Seaweed is a type of algae.

  6. Usually, protists are aquatic.

  7. Protists only reproduce sexually in times of stress and instead does so asexually.
